Platform Events: Track the Publishing of Platform Events with Apex Publish Callbacks (GA)
Contributed by : Subhojeet Ghosh

Whenever you publish a platform event, the publish operation is queued in Salesforce because this is an asynchronous process. When no intermediate errors are returned, you get the queueing result in Database.SaveResult of an Event.publish call. When the resources ara available, the system then carries out the queued publish call.

With this new feature Apex Publish Callbacks, one can also get the final result. After getting the final result then one can decide what action to take next, such as attempting to republish the failed events. From Summer '23, this feature is now Generally Available.
